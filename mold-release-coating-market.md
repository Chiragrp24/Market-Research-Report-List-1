<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Mold Release Coating market includes different types of coatings such as internal mold releases and external mold releases. Internal mold releases are coatings applied on the surface of molds, allowing easy separation of the molded product from the mold. These coatings minimize adhesion, preventing the product from sticking to the mold's surface. On the other hand, external mold releases are coatings applied directly onto the product being molded. These coatings facilitate the removal of the product from the mold, reducing the risk of damage or deformation during the process.</p></p>
