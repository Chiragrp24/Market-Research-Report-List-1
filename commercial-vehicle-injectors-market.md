<p><p>The commercial vehicle injectors market includes two main types: gasoline direct injector and diesel direct injector. Gasoline direct injectors are used in commercial vehicles that are powered by gasoline engines. They directly inject fuel into the combustion chamber, resulting in better fuel efficiency and power. On the other hand, diesel direct injectors are designed for commercial vehicles with diesel engines. They spray fuel directly into the cylinder, ensuring efficient combustion and increased performance. Both types of injectors contribute to the overall functioning and performance of commercial vehicles in their respective fuel types.</p></p>
