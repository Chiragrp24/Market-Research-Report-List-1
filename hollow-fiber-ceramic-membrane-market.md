<p><p>The hollow fiber ceramic membrane market consists of three types: ultrafiltration, microfiltration, and nanofiltration. Ultrafiltration membranes have larger pore sizes, allowing the passage of larger molecules while retaining smaller ones. Microfiltration membranes have even larger pores, enabling the filtration of macro-sized particles. Nanofiltration membranes have smaller pore sizes, selectively removing smaller ions and separating different molecular substances. These technologies find applications in various industries like water treatment, pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and biotechnology, where the specific filtration needs determine the choice of membrane type.</p></p>
