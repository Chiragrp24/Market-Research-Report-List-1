<p><p>Thermochromic pigments are a special type of pigment that change color in response to temperature fluctuations. There are two main types of thermochromic pigments on the market - reversible and irreversible. Reversible thermochromic pigments change color when exposed to heat and revert back to their original color when cooled. On the other hand, irreversible thermochromic pigments permanently change color when heated and cannot revert back to their original color. Both types have various applications in products like paint, coatings, textiles, and toys, offering visual appeal and functionality.</p></p>
