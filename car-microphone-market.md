<p><strong>The Car Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECM Microphone</li><li>MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car microphone market consists of two main types: the ECM microphone and the MEMS microphone. An ECM (Electret Condenser Microphone) is a widely-used type that uses a charged diaphragm and a fixed plate to convert sound into an electrical signal. In contrast, a MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphone utilizes microscopic mechanical structures to capture sound. Both ECM and MEMS microphones have their advantages and are used in various car applications such as hands-free calling or voice command systems.</p></p>
