ZnSe-based Beam Splitters utilize zinc selenide material to divide the infrared light into two beams for analysis, while KBR-based Beam Splitters employ potassium bromide material. Both types of beam splitters play a crucial role in achieving accurate and precise fuel analysis using FTIR technology.</p></p>
