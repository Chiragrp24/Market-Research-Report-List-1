<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Battery Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three types: distributed, centralized, and modular. In a distributed BMS, the battery management functions are distributed across multiple modules within the vehicle, providing independent control and monitoring. In a centralized BMS, all the battery management functions are concentrated in a single module, enhancing system integration. A modular BMS combines both distributed and centralized approaches, allowing flexibility and scalability by providing a combination of centralized control and distributed management across modules.</p></p>
