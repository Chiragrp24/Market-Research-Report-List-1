<p><p>The market for stainless steel round bars can be categorized into two types: hot rolled and cold rolled. Hot rolled bars are created by heating and rolling stainless steel billets, resulting in a rougher surface and a more flexible product suited for construction and industrial applications. Cold rolled bars, on the other hand, are processed at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface and improved dimensional accuracy. They are often used in industries requiring high precision and aesthetic finishes, such as automotive and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
