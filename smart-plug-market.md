<p><p>The Smart Plug market consists of two main types: Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Bluetooth-based smart plugs use short-range wireless technology to connect to and control devices within close proximity. They are suitable for smaller areas like homes or offices. On the other hand, Wi-Fi-based smart plugs utilize a wireless internet connection to control devices from anywhere. They offer wider coverage and are ideal for larger spaces or remote access. Both types enable users to remotely manage and automate their electrical devices for improved convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
