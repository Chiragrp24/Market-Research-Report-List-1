<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking Device (ABS) market can be categorized into different types based on the number of channels it possesses. Single channel ABS uses one sensor to monitor the speed of a single wheel and controls brake pressure accordingly. Double channel ABS consists of two sensors to monitor the speed of both front wheels. Triple channel ABS includes three sensors, monitoring each wheel individually. Other types of ABS may include advanced systems that incorporate additional sensors to monitor multiple parameters for enhanced braking control and safety.</p></p>
