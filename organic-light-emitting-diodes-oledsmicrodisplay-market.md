<p><p>Organic Light-emitting Diodes (OLEDs) Microdisplays are used in various display applications. The Reflective Microdisplay type has a reflective layer that reflects external light, enhancing visibility in bright environments. It utilizes ambient light for illumination, making it suitable for augmented reality applications. On the other hand, Transmissive Microdisplay type allows external light to pass through, and it requires a backlight source for illumination. This type is commonly used in virtual reality headsets and other portable electronic devices where control over external lighting conditions is possible.</p></p>
