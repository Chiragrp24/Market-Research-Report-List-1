<p><ul><li>Roller Gin</li><li>Saw Gin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton gin equipment market consists of two main types: roller gin and saw gin. Roller gin relies on a roller mechanism to separate cotton fibers from the seeds, whereas saw gin uses rotating saw blades to achieve the same result. Roller gin is known for producing high-quality cotton due to its gentle processing, while saw gin is suitable for processing large volumes of cotton quickly. Both types have been widely used in the cotton industry, offering different benefits and meeting varying market demands.</p></p>
