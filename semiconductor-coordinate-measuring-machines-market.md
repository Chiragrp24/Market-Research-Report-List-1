<p><p>Semiconductor Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs) are used for accurate dimensional measurement in the semiconductor industry. Portable CMMs are lightweight and can be easily moved around the manufacturing facility, providing flexibility and convenience for measurement tasks. Desktop CMMs, on the other hand, are stationary machines designed to remain in a fixed location. Although they lack portability, desktop CMMs often offer higher precision and stability due to their fixed setup. Both types of CMMs play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor products by facilitating precise measurements.</p></p>
