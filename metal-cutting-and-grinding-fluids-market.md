Semisynthetic cutting and grinding fluids are a blend of mineral oil and water, offering improved lubrication. Synthetic cutting and grinding fluids are completely oil-free and provide excellent cooling properties. Neat oil cutting and grinding fluids are pure oils that offer high lubrication and heat dissipation.</p></p>
