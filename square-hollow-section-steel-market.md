<p><ul><li>WeldedSHS</li><li>SeamlessSHS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Square Hollow Section Steel (SHS) market consists of two main types: Welded SHS and Seamless SHS. Welded SHS refers to square hollow section steel that is formed by welding flat plates together to create the desired shape. This type is commonly used in construction and fabrication industries due to its affordability and versatility. On the other hand, Seamless SHS refers to square hollow section steel that is manufactured without any welding. This type is known for its superior strength and aesthetic appearance and is often used in high-end applications such as architectural structures.</p></p>
