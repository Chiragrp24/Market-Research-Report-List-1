<p><p>The Gas Chromatograph-Mass Spectrometer (GC-MS) market primarily consists of three types: Gas chromatography with Quadrupole mass spectrometer, Gas chromatography with Time of Flight mass spectrometry, and Gas chromatography with ion trap mass spectrometer. These technologies are used to analyze and identify chemical compounds in various industries like pharmaceutical, environmental, and forensic. Gas chromatography separates the compounds, and the mass spectrometer then detects and measures their mass-to-charge ratio. Each type of GC-MS has unique advantages, such as quadrupole's high sensitivity, time of flight's accurate mass measurement, and ion trap's ability to trap and analyze ions.</p></p>
