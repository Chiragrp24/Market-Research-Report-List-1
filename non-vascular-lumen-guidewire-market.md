<p><p>The non-vascular lumen guidewire market includes two main types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are linear, flexible devices used to access and navigate blood vessels. They provide a straightforward path for medical procedures. On the other hand, elbow guidewires have a curved end that allows for easier vessel access in complex anatomical structures. Both types of guidewires are crucial tools for physicians during minimally invasive procedures that involve navigating and treating non-vascular lumens.</p></p>
