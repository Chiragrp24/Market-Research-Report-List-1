<p><p>The HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) Sensors & Controllers market consists of two main types of devices: sensors and controllers. Sensors are electronic devices designed to detect and measure changes in temperature, humidity, pressure, and other environmental factors in HVAC systems. They provide real-time data to the controllers, which are responsible for regulating the HVAC system's operation based on the information received from the sensors. Controllers make adjustments to maintain optimal conditions, such as adjusting heating or cooling levels, airflow, and humidity. Together, sensors and controllers ensure efficient and precise control of HVAC systems.</p></p>
