<p><p>The air-defense missile system market is categorized into two types: Man-Portable Air-Defense System (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense System. MANPADS are portable missile launchers that can be operated by an individual soldier to engage low-flying aircraft and helicopters. On the other hand, Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems are fixed or mounted on various platforms, such as vehicles, ships, or buildings, providing enhanced protection against airborne threats. Both types serve to safeguard military or civilian targets against aerial attacks, but differ in terms of portability and operational characteristics.</p></p>
