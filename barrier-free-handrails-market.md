<p><p>Barrier-free handrails are horizontal or inclined bars that serve as support or guidance to individuals with reduced mobility, allowing them to move safely and independently. These handrails are designed to be accessible to people with disabilities, including those using wheelchairs or walkers, as well as the elderly and individuals with temporary injuries.</p><p>The market for barrier-free handrails is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The increasing aging population globally, coupled with the rising awareness about accessibility and inclusive infrastructure, is driving the demand for these handrails. Furthermore, government initiatives and regulations mandating the installation of barrier-free facilities in public spaces, buildings, and transportation systems are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has also highlighted the importance of safety and hygiene, further emphasizing the need for barrier-free handrails in public places with high footfall. The market is expected to benefit from the growing focus on public health and safety, as well as the increasing number of construction projects aimed at creating accessible environments.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements are also likely to impact the barrier-free handrails market.
