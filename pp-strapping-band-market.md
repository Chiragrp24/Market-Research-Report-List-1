<p><ul><li>Manual PP Strapping</li><li>Machine Used PP Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PP strapping band market includes two types: manual PP strapping and machine used PP strapping. Manual PP strapping is a type where the band is manually applied by hand to secure packages, pallets, or boxes. It is suitable for small-scale operations or occasional use. On the other hand, machine used PP strapping involves the use of strapping machines to automatically apply and tighten the band around packages, increasing efficiency and productivity. This type is commonly used in large-scale industrial operations that require high volumes of packaging and strapping.</p></p>
