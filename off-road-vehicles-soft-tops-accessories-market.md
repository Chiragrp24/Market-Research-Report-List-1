<p><p>The off-road vehicles soft tops and accessories market can be classified into two types: framed soft tops and frameless soft tops. Framed soft tops have a rigid structure made of metal or fiberglass, providing stability and durability. They usually consist of a fabric cover attached to the frame, offering protection from the elements. On the other hand, frameless soft tops lack a rigid structure and rely on the vehicle's roll cage or straps for support. They provide a more versatile option for off-roading enthusiasts who prefer easy installation and flexibility.</p></p>
