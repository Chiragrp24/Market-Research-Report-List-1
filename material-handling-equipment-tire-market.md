<p><p>Material handling equipment tires are available in three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. </p><p>Pneumatic tires are similar to car tires and are filled with air, providing excellent shock absorption and traction. They are suitable for rough terrains and outdoor use.</p><p>Solid tires are made of solid rubber and are puncture-proof, ensuring durability and long-lasting performance. They are commonly used in warehouses and indoor applications.</p><p>Polyurethane tires are lightweight and offer good traction and load-carrying capacity. They are ideal for delicate surfaces and smooth indoor operations. Each type has its own advantages, allowing businesses to choose based on their specific needs and environments.</p></p>
