<p><ul><li>LEO (Low Earth Orbit)</li><li>MEO (Medium Earth Orbit)</li><li>GEO (Geosynchronous )</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The All-Electric Satellites market comprises three main types: LEO (Low Earth Orbit), MEO (Medium Earth Orbit), and GEO (Geosynchronous) markets. LEO satellites operate at a lower altitude, allowing for quicker data transmission and communication due to shorter distances. MEO satellites, placed at a medium altitude, provide better coverage and higher data rates compared to LEO. GEO satellites orbit at equal speed as Earth's rotation, making them stable over a fixed location. They are commonly used for broadcasting and require higher power levels compared to LEO and MEO satellites.</p></p>
