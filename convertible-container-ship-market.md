<p><p>The Convertible Container Ship market comprises two main types: FCL Container Ships and LCL Container Ships. FCL Container Ships are specially designed to transport Full Container Loads, which means the entire space of a container is utilized by a single buyer. On the other hand, LCL Container Ships are designed to transport Less than Container Loads, where the container space is divided among multiple buyers sharing the same shipment. Both market types cater to different shipping needs, with FCL being more suitable for large-scale shipments and LCL being more flexible for smaller shipments.</p></p>
