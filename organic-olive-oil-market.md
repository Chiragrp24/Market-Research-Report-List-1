<p><p>Organic olive oil is available in three market types: extra virgin olive oil, olive oil, and olive pomace oil. Extra virgin olive oil is the highest quality and most expensive type, obtained directly from olives solely by mechanical means. It has low acidity levels and a distinct flavor. Olive oil, also known as pure olive oil, is a blend of extra virgin olive oil and refined olive oil. It is less expensive and has a milder flavor. Olive pomace oil is made from the residue left after extracting extra virgin oil and refined pomace oil. It is lower in quality and used for cooking purposes.</p></p>
