<p><p>Agricultural motors are categorized into two main types: DC motors and AC motors. DC motors utilize direct current and are commonly used in applications that require variable speed control, such as irrigation systems. They are compact, efficient, and offer high torque. On the other hand, AC motors operate on alternating current and are typically used in fixed-speed applications, such as grain conveyors. While they are less expensive than DC motors, they are larger and less efficient. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different agricultural needs.</p></p>
