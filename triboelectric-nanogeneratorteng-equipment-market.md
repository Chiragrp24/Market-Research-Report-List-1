<p><p>The Triboelectric Nanogenerator (TENG) Equipment market can be categorized into two types - liquid metal based and organic thin film based. Liquid metal based TENG equipment utilizes conductive liquid metals, such as gallium, as the triboelectric materials to generate electricity through friction. On the other hand, organic thin film based TENG equipment employs organic materials, such as polymers or carbon-based compounds, as the triboelectric layers to produce electric power. Both types of TENG equipment are used for harvesting mechanical energy from friction and vibration in various applications.</p></p>
