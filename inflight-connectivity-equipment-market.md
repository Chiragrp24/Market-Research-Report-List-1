Increasing demand for in-flight entertainment and connectivity services is the primary driver behind this growth. BYOD allows passengers to use their personal devices, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops, to access the in-flight connectivity services. This trend is highly favored by airlines as it reduces the cost of installing and maintaining dedicated devices for each passenger.</p><p>The market is witnessing various technological advancements to enhance the inflight connectivity experience.
