<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask inspection equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to identify defects in photomasks, which are crucial components in the manufacturing of semiconductors. There are two main types of inspection methods used: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). Die to Die compares one die on a mask with another die on the same mask, while Die to Database compares the actual die layout on the mask with a reference database. Both methods play a significant role in ensuring the quality and reliability of photomasks, leading to improved semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
