<p><p>The electric patrol robot market includes two main types: wheeled patrol robots and rail-mounted patrol robots. Wheeled patrol robots are designed to move on wheels, providing flexibility and mobility to navigate various terrains. They are commonly used for surveillance and security purposes in areas such as shopping malls and warehouses. On the other hand, rail-mounted patrol robots are fixed on rails, allowing them to operate along a predetermined path. These robots are primarily used in environments with consistent routes, such as train stations, airports, and industrial facilities, for tasks like monitoring and inspection.</p></p>
