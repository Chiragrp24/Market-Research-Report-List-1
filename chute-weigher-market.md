The rising demand for efficient and automated weighing systems in various industries, such as mining, food and beverage, agriculture, and logistics, is a major driving factor for the market. <p><p>Chute weigher market can be categorized into two types: stationary and portable. They provide continuous weighing of materials as they pass through the chute. On the other hand, portable chute weighers are designed to be easily moved from one location to another.
