<p><p>Heat-transfer fluids are substances used in various industries to transfer heat from one location to another. There are two main types of heat-transfer fluids: synthetic organic heat transfer fluids and mineral oils. Synthetic organic heat transfer fluids are made through chemical synthesis and offer several advantages such as higher thermal stability and low environmental impact. On the other hand, mineral oils are derived from petroleum and are more cost-effective but have lower thermal stability. Both types of heat-transfer fluids are widely used depending on the specific requirements and conditions of the application.</p></p>
