<p><p>Aluminum-nickel catalysts are used in various industrial processes to accelerate chemical reactions. Activated catalysts are prepared by modifying the catalyst surface to increase its surface area and reactivity, leading to enhanced catalytic performance. These catalysts offer higher efficiency and selectivity in reactions due to their increased active sites. On the other hand, non-activated catalysts are as-received catalysts without any modification, offering standard catalytic properties.
