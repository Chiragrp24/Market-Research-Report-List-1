<p><p>The light curing acrylic adhesive market is divided into two major types: UV light curing acrylic adhesive and visible light curing acrylic adhesive. UV light curing adhesive is activated by ultraviolet light and is commonly used in applications that require fast curing and high bond strength. Visible light curing adhesive, on the other hand, is activated by visible light and is preferred for applications where UV light is not suitable or accessible. These adhesives offer efficient bonding capabilities and find extensive usage in industries like electronics, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
