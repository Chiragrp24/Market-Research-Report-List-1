<p><p>Smart retail service robots are available in three main market types: rechargeable, plug-in, and swappable. Rechargeable robots are equipped with internal batteries that can be easily charged, allowing them to operate wirelessly for extended periods. Plug-in robots, on the other hand, require a direct power connection to function and do not rely on internal batteries. Swappable robots can have their batteries easily removed and replaced with pre-charged ones, ensuring uninterrupted operation. These market types offer different options for retailers based on their specific needs and preferences in terms of robot power management.</p></p>
