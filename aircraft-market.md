<p><p>The aircraft market is divided into three main types: propeller aircraft, turbo-prop aircraft, and jet aircraft. Propeller aircraft are powered by piston engines and use a propeller to generate thrust. Turbo-prop aircraft combine a turbine engine with a propeller, offering increased power and efficiency. Jet aircraft, on the other hand, use jet engines to produce thrust, resulting in higher speeds and altitudes. These market types cater to different needs and preferences, with propeller aircraft typically favored for shorter distances, turbo-prop aircraft for regional travel, and jet aircraft for long-haul flights.</p></p>
