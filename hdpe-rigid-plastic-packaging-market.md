<p><strong>The HDPE Rigid Plastic Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDPE rigid plastic packaging market offers various types such as extrusion, injection molding, and thermoforming. Extrusion involves melting HDPE pellets and then shaping them into desired products through a die. Injection molding involves injecting molten HDPE into a mold cavity, allowing it to cool and solidify into the desired shape. Thermoforming involves heating a HDPE sheet and shaping it using a mold. Each type offers different advantages and is used for various products like bottles, containers, and trays in the packaging industry.</p></p>
