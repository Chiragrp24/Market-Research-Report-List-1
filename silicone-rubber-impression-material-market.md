<p><p>The silicone rubber impression material market can be categorized into three types: polycondensation, polyether, and others. Polycondensation is a type of silicone rubber that is formed through a chemical reaction between two or more components. Polyether is another type of silicone rubber made by reacting a polyether and a silicone polymer. The "others" category includes various types of silicone rubber impression materials, such as addition-cured silicones or condensation-cured silicones with modified properties. These different types offer varying characteristics and are used in dental and medical applications for making accurate impressions of teeth and tissues.</p></p>
