<p><p>The market for skin ulcers moist dressings products can be classified into four major types: pressure ulcers, diabetic ulcers, venous ulcers, and arterial ulcers. Pressure ulcers are caused by prolonged pressure on the skin, often due to immobility. Diabetic ulcers develop in individuals with diabetes as a result of poor blood circulation and nerve damage. Venous ulcers occur when there is impaired blood flow in the veins, leading to skin breakdown. Arterial ulcers are caused by blocked or narrowed arteries, reducing blood supply to the affected area.</p></p>
