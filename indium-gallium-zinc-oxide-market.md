<p><p>The Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide (IGZO) market can be classified into two types: Crystalline IGZO and Amorphous IGZO. Crystalline IGZO refers to IGZO materials with an ordered atomic structure, resulting in higher electrical conductivity and better performance. On the other hand, Amorphous IGZO lacks a regular arrangement of atoms, leading to lower electrical conductivity. Both types have their own applications and advantages, with Crystalline IGZO being preferred for high-performance devices and Amorphous IGZO being suitable for low-power applications.</p></p>
