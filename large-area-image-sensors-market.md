<p><p>The Large-Area Image Sensors market consists of two main types: CMOS Image Sensors and CCD Image Sensors. CMOS Image Sensors use Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology to convert light into electrical signals, offering low power consumption and high integration capabilities. CCD Image Sensors, on the other hand, employ Charge-Coupled Device technology to capture and transfer electrical charges, providing higher image quality and sensitivity. Both types are widely used in various applications such as smartphones, digital cameras, surveillance systems, and medical imaging devices, driving the growth of the Large-Area Image Sensors market.</p></p>
