<p><p>Electric three wheelers are becoming increasingly popular in the market. There are two main types: side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are electrical motors that are attached to the sides of the vehicle, providing power to the wheels. On the other hand, center motors are situated in the middle of the vehicle, powering the rear wheels. Both types offer unique advantages. Side wheel motors provide better stability and control, while center motors offer better weight distribution and improved traction. Overall, these technologies are revolutionizing the electric three wheeler market.</p></p>
