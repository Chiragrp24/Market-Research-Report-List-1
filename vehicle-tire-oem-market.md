<p><p>The vehicle tire OEM market consists of two main types: radial tire market and bias tire market. Radial tires have cords that run radially from the beads to the tread, allowing for flexible sidewalls and better fuel efficiency. They are known for providing a smooth and comfortable ride. On the other hand, bias tires have cords that crisscross diagonally, providing good load-carrying capacity and durability. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and off-road applications. These market types represent the different manufacturing methods and characteristics of tires used in the automotive industry.</p></p>
