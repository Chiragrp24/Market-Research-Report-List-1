<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminate tube packaging is available in two primary types: ABL (aluminum barrier laminate) and PBL (plastic barrier laminate). ABL tubes consist of several layers, including an aluminum layer that provides exceptional barrier properties against light, moisture, and gases. PBL tubes, on the other hand, feature multiple layers of plastic materials that offer effective barrier properties. Both types are widely used in various industries, such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food, due to their ability to protect the contents, prevent contamination, and enhance shelf life.</p></p>
