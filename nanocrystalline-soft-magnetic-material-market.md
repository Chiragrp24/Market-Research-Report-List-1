The increasing demand for efficient and compact electrical devices in various industries, such as electronics, automotive, and energy, has driven the adoption of nanocrystalline soft magnetic materials. These materials offer high saturation induction, low core losses, and excellent thermal stability, making them ideal for applications such as inductors, transformers, and magnetic shields.</p><p>Moreover, the growing focus on renewable energy and the need for energy-efficient power systems have further boosted the demand for nanocrystalline soft magnetic materials.
