<p><p>Modified bitumen is a type of asphalt used in various construction applications. There are different types of modified bitumen, including SBS modified bitumen, which is made using Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene polymers to enhance its flexibility and durability. Plastomer modified bitumen incorporates additional plastomer polymers for improved resistance to aging and cracking. Crumb Rubber modified bitumen is made by adding recycled rubber crumbs, enhancing its elasticity and reducing environmental impact. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various modified bitumen types that may utilize different additives or methods to enhance specific properties.</p></p>
