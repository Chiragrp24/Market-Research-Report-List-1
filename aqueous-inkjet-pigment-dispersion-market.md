<p><p>Aqueous inkjet pigment dispersions are used in inkjet printing to provide color and stability to ink formulations. The market for these dispersions can be categorized into different types based on the type of binder used. Oil-based dispersions use oil as a binder, providing good adhesion and durability. Alkyd dispersions use alkyd resins, offering high gloss and fast drying properties. Epoxy dispersions utilize epoxy resins for excellent chemical resistance and adhesion. The "Others" category includes various other binder types such as acrylics, urethanes, and vinyls, each offering unique properties for specific inkjet applications.</p></p>
