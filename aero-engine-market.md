<p><p>The Aero-Engine market consists of two main types: Piston Engine and Gas Turbine Engine markets. Piston engines are internal combustion engines that use reciprocating motion to convert fuel into mechanical energy. They are commonly used in small aircraft and offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, gas turbine engines use a continuous combustion process to convert fuel into mechanical energy. They are commonly used in larger aircraft and offer higher power output and efficiency. Both markets cater to different aircraft sizes and application requirements.</p></p>
