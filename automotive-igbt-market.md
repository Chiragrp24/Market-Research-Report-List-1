<p><p>The automotive IGBT market is divided into various types, namely CIB/PIM (Converter-Inverter-Brake/Power Integrated Module), and IPM (Intelligent Power Module). CIB/PIM modules are compact and versatile, integrating converter, inverter, brake, and power functionalities into a single module. They are suitable for electric vehicles, hybrid electric vehicles, and other automotive applications. On the other hand, IPMs are advanced modules that combine IGBTs, gate drivers, and protection circuits. They provide enhanced reliability, lower power losses, and increased efficiency. IPMs are commonly used in electric vehicle motor drives and other high-power applications.</p></p>
