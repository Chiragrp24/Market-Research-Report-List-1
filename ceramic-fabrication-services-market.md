<p><p>Ceramic fabrication services market encompasses various types of processes for manufacturing ceramic products. Casting involves pouring liquid ceramic material into a mold to create the desired shape. Grinding involves using abrasive tools to shape and refine the ceramic surface. Injection molding utilizes high-pressure to inject molten ceramic into a mold, resulting in intricate shapes. Machining involves removing excess ceramic material through cutting, drilling, or milling. Other markets include processes like pressing, sintering, and extrusion. These different techniques cater to diverse needs, enabling the production of a wide range of ceramic products.</p></p>
