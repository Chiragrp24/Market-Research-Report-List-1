<p><strong>The Cargo Bike Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Tire</li><li>Tubeless Tire</li><li>Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cargo bike tire market includes three types of tires: tube tires, tubeless tires, and solid tires. Tube tires are traditional tires that require an inner tube to hold the air pressure. Tubeless tires, on the other hand, do not require inner tubes and have a special airtight design. They offer advantages such as lower puncture risk and better traction. Solid tires, as the name suggests, do not have air-filled tubes. They are puncture-proof but provide less cushioning compared to tube or tubeless tires. Each type has its own benefits and considerations for cargo bike usage.</p></p>
