<p><p>Powder adhesive refers to a type of adhesive that is available in the form of a powder. There are two main types of powder adhesives: soluble powder adhesive and hot-melt powder adhesive. Soluble powder adhesive dissolves in water, making it easy to apply and remove. It is commonly used in industries such as packaging and paper processing. Hot-melt powder adhesive, on the other hand, is melted and applied in its liquid state, bonding materials together as it cools. It is commonly used in industries such as woodworking and textiles.</p></p>
