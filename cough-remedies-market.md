<p><p>The cough remedies market comprises several types of medications that are commonly used to treat cough symptoms. Antihistamines are drugs that help alleviate coughs caused by allergies by reducing the body's allergic response. Expectorants are medications that thin mucus and make it easier to cough up. Bronchodilators help relax and widen the airways in the lungs, making breathing easier. Decongestants are used to relieve nasal congestion associated with coughs. Antibiotics are prescribed when a cough is caused by a bacterial infection, helping to fight off the infection and reduce cough symptoms.</p></p>
