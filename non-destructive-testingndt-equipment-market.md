<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment Market Types comprise of various techniques used to examine and inspect materials and components without causing any damage. Eddy Current Testing involves inducing electrical currents to detect defects or flaws on surfaces of conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing uses magnets to identify flaws and irregularities in ferromagnetic materials. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects in various materials. Lastly, there are other methods such as X-ray radiography, visual inspection, and thermal imaging, which are also used for non-destructive testing purposes.</p></p>
