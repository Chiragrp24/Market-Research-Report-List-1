<p><p>Roadway gratings are steel or aluminum structures used in roadways and sidewalks to cover drains and manholes. They play a crucial role in managing surface water drainage and preventing accidents caused by uneven surfaces.</p><p>The current outlook for the roadway gratings market is positive, with a steady growth rate expected in the coming years. The increasing urbanization and infrastructure development across the globe are driving the demand for roadway gratings. Governments and municipal corporations are investing heavily in improving transportation infrastructure, which includes the installation of new roadways and the maintenance of existing ones. This is further supplemented by the growing awareness about the importance of proper surface water drainage to avoid flooding and waterlogging.</p><p>Additionally, safety regulations and standards are becoming more stringent, encouraging the use of high-quality roadway gratings that can withstand heavy traffic loads and provide adequate slip resistance.
