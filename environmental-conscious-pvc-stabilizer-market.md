<p><p>Environmental conscious PVC stabilizers are additives used in the production of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) to enhance its thermal and mechanical properties while minimizing its impact on the environment. One of the key driving factors is the increasing awareness about the adverse effects of lead exposure and the stringent government regulations restricting the use of lead-based stabilizers. As a result, manufacturers are focusing on developing and commercializing alternative stabilizers that comply with environmental regulations.</p><p>Moreover, the growing demand for PVC in various end-use industries such as building and construction, automotive, electrical and electronics, and packaging is also expected to drive market growth.
