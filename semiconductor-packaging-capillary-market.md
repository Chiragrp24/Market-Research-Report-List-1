<p><p>Semiconductor packaging capillaries are used to transfer liquid materials during the packaging process. There are three main types: tungsten, titanium, and ceramic capillaries. Tungsten capillaries are widely used due to their excellent durability and high melting point. Titanium capillaries offer good resistance to chemical reactions and are suitable for corrosive liquids. Ceramic capillaries provide high chemical resistance and stability at high temperatures. Each type has specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the packaging process.</p></p>
