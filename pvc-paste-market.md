<p><strong>The PVC Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC paste market consists of two types: Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. In the Micro Suspension Method, tiny PVC particles are suspended in a liquid medium and then blended together to form a paste. This method allows for a high degree of control over the particle size and distribution. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves the use of emulsifiers to blend PVC resin with water. This method produces a fine, stable PVC paste suitable for various applications. Both methods are widely used in the PVC paste market.</p></p>
