<p><p>The adjustable power hospital bed market encompasses two main types: full electric hospital beds and semi-electric hospital beds. Full electric hospital beds are equipped with electric motors that allow the adjustment of the bed's height, backrest, and footrest using a remote control. They provide users with greater comfort and convenience as they can easily change their position without assistance. On the other hand, semi-electric hospital beds have electrically-operated height adjustment, while the backrest and footrest need to be manually adjusted. This type offers a balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
