<p><p>The ocean current energy electric generator market consists of two main types: horizontal shaft generators and vertical shaft generators. A horizontal shaft generator uses a turbine with blades positioned horizontally in the water current to generate electricity. On the other hand, a vertical shaft generator has blades positioned vertically to harness the energy from the ocean current. Both types of generators aim to convert the kinetic energy of the ocean current into electrical energy, offering potential opportunities for renewable and sustainable power generation.</p></p>
