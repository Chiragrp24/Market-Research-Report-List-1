<p><p>A Blood Glucose Monitoring System (BGMS) is a medical device used by individuals with diabetes to measure their blood glucose levels. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of diabetes, rising awareness about the importance of regular blood glucose monitoring, and technological advancements in the devices.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the BGMS market is segmented by product type, distribution channel, and geography. The self-monitoring blood glucose systems segment dominates the market due to its ease of use and widespread adoption.</p><p>By distribution channel, the market is categorized into hospital pharmacies, retail pharmacies, and online sales.
