The under 100 cps market includes adhesives with low viscosity that rapidly penetrate and bond materials, making them ideal for delicate applications. The 100-1000 cps market offers adhesives with medium viscosity suitable for a range of bonding applications. The above 1000 cps market provides high viscosity adhesives that create strong, durable bonds for more demanding and load-bearing applications.</p></p>
