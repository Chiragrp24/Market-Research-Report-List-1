<p><p>The double-spindle turning center market consists of two main types: vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical turning centers have a vertical orientation, where the spindle is positioned vertically and the workpiece is mounted on a rotary table or fixture below. This configuration is ideal for heavy-duty machining applications and offers high stability and precision. On the other hand, horizontal turning centers have a horizontal spindle orientation, with the workpiece mounted on a rotating chuck. Horizontal machines are commonly used for high-volume production and provide excellent accessibility for loading and unloading operations.</p></p>
