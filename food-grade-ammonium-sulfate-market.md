<p><p>Food Grade Ammonium Sulfate is a white crystalline solid that is commonly used as a food additive. It is denoted by the chemical formula (NH4)2SO4 and is composed of two ammonium ions (NH4+) and one sulfate ion (SO42-). It is primarily used as a nitrogen source in fertilizers but is also approved by regulatory bodies such as the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for use in food products.</p><p>Food Grade Ammonium Sulfate is utilized in various food applications, such as bread and bakery products, processed meats, dairy products, and beverages. It helps to improve dough properties, control pH levels, enhance protein structure, and prolong shelf life by preventing the growth of spoilage microorganisms. The rise in demand for processed and convenience foods, along with the increasing population, is driving the growth of the Food Grade Ammonium Sulfate market.</p><p>The market for Food Grade Ammonium Sulfate is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.6% during the forecast period.
