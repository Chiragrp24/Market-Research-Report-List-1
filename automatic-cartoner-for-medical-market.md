<p><p>In the medical market, there are two types of automatic cartoners: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic cartoners are designed to efficiently and completely automate the cartoning process. They can handle a high volume of products and perform tasks such as product loading, carton forming, and carton closing without human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic cartoners still require some manual intervention. While they can perform essential tasks automatically, they require human assistance in tasks like product loading or manually closing the cartons. Both types offer advantages based on production volume and level of automation required.</p></p>
