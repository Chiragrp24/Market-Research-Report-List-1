<p><strong>The Medical Grade Surge Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Grade Surge Protector market can be classified into three types: voltage switch type, pressure limiting type, and combination type. The voltage switch type surge protectors are designed to automatically switch to a safe voltage level during power surges. Pressure limiting type surge protectors regulate the flow of electrical pressure and prevent it from exceeding safe limits. Combination type surge protectors combine both voltage switching and pressure limiting capabilities, providing comprehensive protection against surges in medical equipment. These different types cater to diverse protection needs in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
