<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home wind turbine market consists of two main types: horizontal-axis wind turbines (HAWTs) and vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWTs). HAWTs are the most common type and have blades that rotate horizontally, facing the wind. They are efficient, especially in higher wind speeds, and provide a higher power output. On the other hand, VAWTs have blades that rotate vertically, which enables them to capture wind from any direction. They are suitable for areas with lower wind speeds and limited space, making them a popular choice for residential use.</p></p>
