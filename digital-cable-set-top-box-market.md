<p><p>The digital cable set-top box market can be broadly classified into two categories: Over the Top (OTT) and Direct Terrestrial Transmission (DTT). OTT refers to the delivery of media content, such as video and audio, directly over the internet without the involvement of traditional cable or satellite providers. On the other hand, DTT refers to the transmission of digital signals over the airwaves using terrestrial broadcast towers. Both types provide consumers with access to a wide range of content, but OTT relies on internet connectivity while DTT uses traditional broadcasting technology.</p></p>
