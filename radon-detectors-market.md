<p><p>Radon detectors are devices used to measure the levels of radon gas in indoor environments. There are two main types of radon detectors available in the market: long-term and short-term detectors. </p><p>Long-term detectors are designed to measure radon levels over an extended period, usually between three months to one year. They provide a more accurate representation of the average radon levels in a building.</p><p>On the other hand, short-term detectors are used for faster results, typically measuring radon levels from a few days up to three months. Although they may provide quicker insights, they are less precise and may not capture variations in radon levels over time as effectively as long-term detectors.</p></p>
