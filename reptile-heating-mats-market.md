<p><p>Reptile heating mats are available in two main types: adhesive heating mats and non-adhesive heating mats. Adhesive heating mats have a sticky backing that allows them to be attached directly to the enclosure or tank floor of reptiles. This type is convenient for small reptile enclosures or uneven surfaces. On the other hand, non-adhesive heating mats are designed to be placed underneath the reptile's enclosure without any stickiness. These mats provide a flexible heating solution and can be easily moved or adjusted as needed.</p></p>
