<p><p>Marine Incinerators are used for waste management on ships and offshore platforms. There are two types: Horizontal Marine Incinerators and Vertical Cyclone Marine Incinerators. Horizontal Marine Incinerators have a horizontal combustion chamber and are suitable for burning solid and liquid waste. Vertical Cyclone Marine Incinerators have a vertical combustion chamber with a cyclone system that efficiently burns solid waste. Both types aid in reducing waste volume, ensuring environmental compliance, and promoting safe waste disposal at sea.</p></p>
