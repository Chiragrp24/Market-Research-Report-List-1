<p><p>EPB Motor market types include DC Brushed Motor and DC Brushless Motor. A DC Brushed Motor consists of a permanent magnet and rotating armature, with brushes creating electrical contact, providing simplicity and low cost. On the other hand, a DC Brushless Motor replaces the brushes with electronic commutation, offering higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance. Both motor types serve various applications such as automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, with DC Brushless Motors gradually gaining popularity due to their superior performance and environmental benefits.</p></p>
