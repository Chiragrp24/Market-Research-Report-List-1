<p><p>Blocking oil type copper terminals are a type of electrical connector that is filled with blocking oil to enhance its performance and durability. The blocking oil helps to prevent moisture or other contaminants from entering the terminal, ensuring better insulation and reducing the risk of short circuits or damage. On the other hand, tube pressure type copper terminals are designed with a hollow tube that can be compressed to tightly secure the wire or cable, providing a reliable connection that can withstand high levels of pressure and vibrations. Both types offer unique advantages for various electrical applications.</p></p>
