<p><p>The rail wheel and axle market can be classified into two types: rolled wheels and axles for railways, and forged wheels and axles for railways. Rolled wheels and axles are produced through a rolling process, where the metal is passed through rollers to form the desired shape. This method is cost-effective for mass production. On the other hand, forged wheels and axles are made by applying extreme heat and pressure to shape the metal.
