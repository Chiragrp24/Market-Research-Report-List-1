<p><p>V-Cell filters are available in three different market types: 3 V-Cell, 4 V-Cell, and 5 V-Cell. These market types refer to the number of V-shaped pleats present in the filter. In a 3 V-Cell filter, there are three pleats, while a 4 V-Cell filter has four pleats, and a 5 V-Cell filter contains five pleats. The number of pleats in the filter affects its surface area and efficiency in capturing particles. The higher the number of V-Cells, the larger the surface area and better the filtration capability.</p></p>
