<p><p>Micromobility telematics system refers to a technology-based solution that enables tracking and monitoring of small electric vehicles used for short-distance traveling, such as e-bikes, e-scooters, and electric skateboards. The increasing demand for sustainable transportation solutions, coupled with the growing popularity of e-bikes and e-scooters as convenient alternatives to traditional commuting methods, drives the market growth. Moreover, the integration of advanced features such as remote diagnostics, predictive maintenance, and fleet management capabilities further enhances the value proposition of micromobility telematics systems.</p><p>In addition, the adoption of these systems by ride-sharing and rental service providers is anticipated to fuel market expansion. Micromobility telematics systems enable these companies to better manage their fleets, prevent theft, optimize charging and maintenance schedules, and improve overall operational efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of micromobility telematics with smart city infrastructure and emerging technologies like internet of things (IoT) and artificial intelligence (AI) presents significant growth opportunities. The ability to leverage real-time data from these systems can contribute to smarter urban planning, traffic management, and enhanced safety for micromobility users.</p><p>However, the market also faces some challenges.
