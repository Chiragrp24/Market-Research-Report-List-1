<p><p>The Process Gas Reciprocating Compressors market consists of two types: single stage and multi-stage compressors. Single stage compressors are designed to compress gas in one stage, meaning that they directly compress the gas from its initial pressure to the desired final pressure. On the other hand, multi-stage compressors compress gas in multiple stages, with each stage bringing the gas to an intermediate pressure before it moves on to the next stage. This allows for higher pressure ratios and better efficiency than single stage compressors for certain applications.</p></p>
