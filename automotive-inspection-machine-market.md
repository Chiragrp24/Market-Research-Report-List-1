<p><p>The automotive inspection machine market includes three main types: engine analyzer, analog camera, and code reader types. Engine analyzers are used to diagnose and analyze the performance of engines in vehicles. Analog cameras capture real-time images and videos of the automotive parts, aiding in the inspection process. Code readers are designed to retrieve diagnostic trouble codes from a vehicle's computer system, allowing technicians to identify and resolve issues. These different types cater to the diverse needs of automotive inspection, providing efficient solutions for diagnosing and maintaining vehicles.</p></p>
