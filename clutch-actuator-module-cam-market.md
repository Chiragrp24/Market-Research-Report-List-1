<p><p>The Clutch Actuator Module (CAM) market consists of four types: Manual Transmission, Automatic Transmission, Automated Manual Transmission, and Continuously Variable Transmission. Manual Transmission refers to vehicles with a clutch pedal, requiring the driver to manually engage/disengage gears. Automatic Transmission vehicles don't have a clutch pedal, as the transmission automatically shifts gears. Automated Manual Transmission combines the features of manual and automatic transmissions, allowing for both manual and automatic gear shifting. Continuously Variable Transmission uses a belt-and-pulley system to provide seamless gear ratio adjustment, enhancing fuel efficiency and smooth acceleration.</p></p>
