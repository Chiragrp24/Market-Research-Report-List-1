<p><p>The synthetic wood adhesives market can be categorized into four types. Solvent-based adhesives are made by dissolving synthetic polymers in organic solvents, offering strong bonding capabilities. Water-based adhesives, on the other hand, are created by combining synthetic polymers in water, making them eco-friendly and easy to use. Solventless adhesives do not contain any solvents, making them safer to handle and reducing environmental impact. The "Others" category includes specialty adhesives with unique formulations or properties that may cater to specific adhesive requirements in the wood industry.</p></p>
