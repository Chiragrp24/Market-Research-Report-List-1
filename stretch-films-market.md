<p><p>Stretch films are widely used for packaging and securing goods for transportation or storage. There are three main types of stretch films in the market: Polyethylene (PE), Polypropylene (PP), and Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC). PE films are highly flexible and commonly used for wrapping pallets or bundling smaller items. PP films offer excellent clarity and tear resistance, making them ideal for securing heavy loads. PVC films are known for their superior strength and durability, often utilized for wrapping irregularly shaped or sharp-edged products. Each type has specific characteristics that cater to various packaging needs.</p></p>
