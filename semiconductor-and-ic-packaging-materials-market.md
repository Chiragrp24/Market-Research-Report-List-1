<p><p>The Semiconductor and IC Packaging Materials market encompasses various types of materials used in the packaging of semiconductors and integrated circuits (ICs). Organic substrates refer to materials like plastics or resins that provide a base for mounting components. Bonding wires are used to connect different parts of the package. Leadframes are metal frames that support the IC. Ceramic packages are made of ceramic materials and are used for high-performance ICs. Solder balls are small spheres of solder used for connecting the IC to the package. "Others" refer to additional materials like encapsulants or sealants used in packaging.</p></p>
