<p><p>UVC LEDs (Ultraviolet C Light Emitting Diodes) are small semiconductor devices that emit ultraviolet light in the wavelength range of 200 to 280 nanometers. UVC LEDs are utilized in various industries including healthcare, water purification, food and beverage processing, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p><p>The future outlook of the UVC LEDs market is highly favorable, mainly driven by the increasing demand for effective and efficient disinfection solutions.
