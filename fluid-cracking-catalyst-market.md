There are two types of FCC catalysts: amorphous aluminum silicate and crystalline aluminum silicate. Amorphous aluminum silicate catalysts have a disordered atomic structure, providing higher activity and selectivity. Crystalline aluminum silicate catalysts have an ordered atomic structure, offering improved stability and long-term performance. Both types are used in various FCC processes depending on the desired outcomes of efficiency, product yield, and quality.</p></p>
