<p><strong>The Flat Screen Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat screen printing machines can be categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I machines are designed for printing on flat surfaces such as paper, textiles, and plastics. They offer high precision and accurate color registration, making them suitable for applications like textile printing and PCB manufacturing. On the other hand, Type II machines are specifically designed for printing on curved or irregular surfaces, like bottles or cylindrical objects. They have specialized fixtures and mechanisms to ensure smooth and even printing on these challenging surfaces.</p></p>
