<p><p>The Clinical Oncology Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) market includes various types. NGS Pre-Sequencing involves sample preparation, library construction, and target enrichment. Sequencing involves the actual sequencing of DNA or RNA samples. NGS Data Analysis comprises the interpretation of the resulting sequencing data. Primary Data Analysis includes base calling and quality control. Secondary Data Analysis involves alignment, variant calling, and annotation. Finally, Tertiary Data Analysis integrates the results with clinical information and databases for clinical decision-making. These various stages collectively contribute to the comprehensive and accurate analysis of genomic data in clinical oncology.</p></p>
