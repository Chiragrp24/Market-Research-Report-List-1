<p><p>The PET-CT scanning services market comprises two main types of scanners: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanners and X-Ray Computed Tomography (CT) scanners. PET scanners use a radioactive tracer to produce 3D images of the body's metabolic activity, allowing for the detection and diagnosis of diseases like cancer. CT scanners utilize X-rays to generate detailed cross-sectional images, aiding in the detection of tumors, fractures, and other abnormalities. Both scanners play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions, contributing to the growth of the PET-CT scanning services market.</p></p>
