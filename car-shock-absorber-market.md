<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The car shock absorber market can be categorized based on two factors: structure and control method. In terms of structure, there are twin-tube type and mono-tube type shock absorbers. Twin-tube type has two tubes, with an inner one for fluid displacement and an outer one for gas storage. Mono-tube type, on the other hand, has a single tube for both fluid and gas. In terms of control method, there are hydraulic type and pneumatic type shock absorbers. Hydraulic type uses hydraulic fluid to dampen vibrations, while pneumatic type utilizes air pressure for the same purpose.</p></p>
