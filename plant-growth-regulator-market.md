<p><p>The Plant Growth Regulator market includes various types of regulators that assist in the growth and development of plants. Cytokinins are responsible for cell division and promote plant growth. Auxins play a crucial role in controlling plant growth, particularly in stem elongation and root development. Gibberellins are essential for stimulating seed germination and fruit development. Ethylene or Ethylene Releasers regulate various physiological processes like fruit ripening and senescence. Mepiquat Chloride is a growth regulator that controls plant height and improves yields by inhibiting gibberellin synthesis.</p></p>
