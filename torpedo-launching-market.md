<p><p>There are two main market types for torpedo launching systems: underwater tubes and deck-mounted units. Underwater tubes, as the name suggests, are installed beneath the waterline of naval vessels. They provide protection and concealment for torpedoes, enabling secretive and efficient launches. On the other hand, deck-mounted units are located on the deck of ships and submarines. These units are more easily accessible for maintenance and loading, allowing for quicker torpedo deployment. Both types offer different advantages, and their usage depends on the specific requirements of the naval force.</p></p>
