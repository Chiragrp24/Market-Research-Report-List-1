<p><p>The Full Automatic Surface Grinder market is classified into three main types: Saddle Type, Column Type, and Others. Saddle Type grinders feature a cross-moving saddle on the worktable, offering high efficiency and accuracy in grinding operations. Column Type grinders have a vertical grinding wheel spindle that moves vertically along the column, allowing for enhanced stability and grinding precision. The "Others" category includes various types of surface grinders not falling into the aforementioned types. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market, providing a wide range of options to customers.</p></p>
