<p><p>The UTV tire market encompasses three main types of tires: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a comfortable and smoother ride, while also offering good traction and shock absorption. Solid tires are made from a rubber compound and do not require air, making them puncture-proof and low maintenance, but they may provide a less comfortable ride.
