<p><p>The Electrical Oil Pump market involves two main types: Separate Pumps and Integrated Pumps. Separate Pumps refer to a system where the oil pump is separate from the main engine or motor, operating independently. This type allows for flexibility in installation and maintenance. On the other hand, Integrated Pumps are incorporated directly into the main engine or motor, making them more compact and efficient. They eliminate the need for additional space and connections, resulting in simplified design and reduced costs. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
