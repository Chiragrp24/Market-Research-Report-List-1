<p><p>The multiaxis CNC machine market is categorized into four types based on the number of axes they can move along. A 2-axis CNC machine can move in two directions: X and Y. A 3-axis CNC machine operates in 3 axes: X, Y, and Z, enabling vertical movement. A 4-axis CNC machine can rotate and tilt the workpiece in addition to the 3-axis movements. Finally, a 5-axis CNC machine can move the workpiece in five axes, including rotating along the X and Y axes, allowing for more complex and precise operations.</p></p>
