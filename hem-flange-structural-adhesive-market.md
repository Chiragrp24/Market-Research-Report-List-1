<p><p>The Hem Flange Structural Adhesive market can be categorized into two types: One Component and Two Component. One Component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready for immediate use, eliminating the need for additional mixing. They are convenient and offer faster bonding times. Two Component adhesives require mixing of two separate components before application. This allows for longer working time and the ability to adjust adhesive properties. Both types provide excellent bonding strength and durability in joining hem flanges, which are typically used in automotive and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
