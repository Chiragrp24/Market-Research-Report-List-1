<p><p>Nonwoven fabrics market is divided into several types based on manufacturing processes. Dry-laid nonwoven fabrics are produced by entangling fibers with mechanical, thermal, or chemical techniques. Spunmelt nonwovens are made by extruding and bonding fibers together. Wet-laid nonwovens involve suspending fibers in a solution, forming them into a sheet, and then bonding them together. Other types in the nonwoven fabrics market include needlepunch, meltblown, and airlaid. These versatile fabrics find applications in several industries such as automotive, healthcare, and construction due to their superior properties like flexibility, strength, and absorbency.</p></p>
