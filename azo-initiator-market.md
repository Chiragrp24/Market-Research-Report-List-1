<p><ul><li>Bulk Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li><li>Solution Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The azo-initiator market finds application in various polymerization processes like bulk, suspension, and solution polymerization. In bulk polymerization, the azo-initiator helps initiate the reaction in a homogeneous mixture of monomers without the presence of a solvent. In suspension polymerization, the azo-initiator is used in conjunction with a dispersant to produce polymers with particle sizes suspended in a liquid medium. In solution polymerization, the azo-initiator facilitates the reaction between monomers in a solution, resulting in the formation of polymers dissolved in the solvent.</p></p>
