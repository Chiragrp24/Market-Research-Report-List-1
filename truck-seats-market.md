<p><p>The truck seats market consists of two main types: air suspension truck seats and mechanical suspension truck seats. Air suspension truck seats utilize air-filled cushions to provide a smooth and comfortable ride. They are designed to absorb shocks and vibrations, ensuring reduced driver fatigue. On the other hand, mechanical suspension truck seats use springs and shock absorbers to support the driver's weight, providing a stable and supportive seating experience. Both types of seats aim to enhance the driver's comfort and safety during long hauls and off-road travels.</p></p>
