<p><p>Silicone in construction refers to the various types of silicone materials used in the industry. Fluids are silicone liquids with low viscosity, often used for sealing and lubricating applications. Elastomers are flexible silicone materials that provide durability and elasticity, commonly used for waterproofing and insulation purposes. Resins are solid silicone materials used for encapsulation and adhesive purposes. Gels are semi-solid silicone materials with high viscosity, used for sealing gaps and providing vibration damping. These different silicone types cater to diverse construction needs, offering versatility and reliability.</p></p>
