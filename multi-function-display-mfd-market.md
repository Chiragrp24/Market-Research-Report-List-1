<p><p>The Multi-Function Display (MFD) market consists of different types of display technologies such as LED, Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), LCD Monitor, Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display (AMLCD), and Thin Film Transistor (TFT). LED displays use light-emitting diodes to provide high brightness, energy efficiency, and color contrast. OLED displays use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, offering better display quality and flexibility. LCD Monitors use liquid crystals to modulate light and provide sharper images. AMLCD and TFT displays incorporate active matrix technology for faster response times and improved image quality.</p></p>
