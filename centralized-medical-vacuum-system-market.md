<p><p>The Centralized Medical Vacuum System market includes three types: Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. Dry Claw Pump Technology relies on rotating claws to compress air without using oil, making it more environmentally friendly. Dry Rotary Vane Technology uses vanes to compress air while maintaining a dry atmosphere, which enhances the efficiency of the system. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology employs oil for lubrication and sealing purposes to facilitate efficient air compression, making it a common choice for medical vacuum systems.</p></p>
