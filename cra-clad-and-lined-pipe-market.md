<p><p>CRA (Corrosion Resistant Alloy) clad and lined pipes are types of pipes used in various industries to combat corrosion and prolong the lifespan of pipelines. CRA clad pipe consists of a layer of CRA material bonded to the outer surface of the pipe, while CRA lined pipe has a layer of CRA material lining the inner surface of the pipe. Both types provide corrosion resistance, with clad pipe offering protection from external corrosion and lined pipe providing a barrier against internal corrosion. These market types cater to industries where corrosion protection is essential, such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation.</p></p>
