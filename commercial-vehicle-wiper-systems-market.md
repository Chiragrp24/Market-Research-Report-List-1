<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Wiper Systems refer to the mechanisms installed in vehicles to clear windshields during adverse weather conditions. The market is categorized into various types, including Optical Sensor, Capacitive Sensor, and Others. Optical Sensor systems utilize optical technology to detect rain or moisture on the windshield and activate the wipers accordingly. Capacitive Sensor systems work based on the change in capacitance caused by moisture or water droplets on the windshield. The category "Others" encompasses various alternative or combined sensor technologies used in commercial vehicle wiper systems.</p></p>
